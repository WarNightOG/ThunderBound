History of this Journey

This legendary tale begins in 2019 during the infamous coronavirus era when the world hit pause and everyone was stuck at home binge-watching TV shows or discovering hidden talents like baking really bad banana bread. But for me, something truly epic was about to unfold. Enter Four Legends SMP, a Minecraft survival multiplayer server that brought light to those dark times. It was created by none other than Hassan Javed, also known as WarNightOG. I wasn’t alone in this adventure; I was joined by my two amazing cousins, Maryam also known as Silent_Girl or Pro_Devil_Girl, AzkaRabeeb also known as Queen_Girl, and believe it or not, my own arch-nemesis, my brother. Together we did things no one had ever done before. We built, we fought, we conquered, and most importantly, we had a blast. To this day, I still miss that time. As time passed, I, Hassan Javed, WarNightOG, grew up and decided to try my hand at making websites. I started with Google Sites and WordPress, and while I did find some success, let’s just say I wasn’t exactly launching rockets into space. But one thing was certain, I was learning a lot. I began understanding how the vast universe of the internet worked, unraveling the algorithms of YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, and more. The deeper I dived, the more fascinated I became. Then, a major turning point arrived. In July 2023, my curiosity led me to a Python course by CodeWithHarry. I started learning and completed the first two chapters but quickly realized something, this wasn’t enough for me. My vision was much bigger. So, I switched gears and took on his Full Web Development Course, grinding through the first 30 videos like a warrior on a coding quest. That’s when I met my three legendary companions, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Then came the next big challenge, school exams. These weren’t just any exams, they were from United Charter School, a place as tough as Beaconhouse. But guess what, I crushed them. After emerging victorious, the summer holidays began, giving me precious time to level up my coding skills. Despite my passion, I felt alone in my programming journey. My brothers constantly mocked me, saying things like, Coding is useless, just play games instead. It was tough to hear, but I refused to let their negativity shape my destiny. Instead, I embraced the struggle. I coded through midnights, evenings, and countless exhausting hours, feeling like I was battling an invisible enemy. But then, a guiding light emerged, one that only a true Ninjago fan would understand. When there is no calm outside of you, you must find the calm inside of you. That wisdom from Master Wu carried me through the darkest moments. I learned that the taste of patience is sweeter than the muddiest obstacles life throws at you. Fast forward to 2024, I had mastered website development, AI bots, and even server management. I applied everything I learned, experimenting with new ideas and pushing my limits. And now in 2025, the website you are reading this on, it was built by my very own hands. But this is just the beginning. I am embarking on a new journey, one I call Fog into the Unknown. This journey will help me grow even further, but no matter how far I go, I will never forget the struggles, the learning, and the moments that shaped me. So, if you ever feel lost, overwhelmed, or surrounded by chaos, just remember this, when there is no calm outside of you, you must find the calm inside of you. And who knows, maybe one day your own legendary story will begin.
